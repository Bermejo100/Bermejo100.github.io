# Privacy Policy for Tiktok Automation

**Effective Date:** May 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Bermejo1'' ("we," "us," or "our") handles your information when you use our content management tool, Tiktok Automation (the "Application"), to publish videos to TikTok.

## 1. Information We Collect

When you authenticate the Application with your TikTok account, we receive the following information via the TikTok API:
*   **Authorized Scopes:** We collect confirmation of the permissions you grant us (e.g., `video.upload`, `video.publish`).
*   **User ID:** A unique identifier provided by TikTok to associate the Application with your account.

We **DO NOT** collect or store:
*   Your TikTok username, password, or login credentials.
*   Your personal data (email, phone number, location).
*   The content of the videos you publish (we only process them for upload).

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purpose:
*   To enable the core functionality of the Application: uploading and publishing videos created by you to your authorised TikTok account.

We will **NEVER**:
*   Sell, rent, or lease your information to third parties.
*   Post content to your TikTok account without your explicit action within the Application.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

*   **Temporary Processing:** Your videos are processed locally on your hardware. We do not store them on any external servers.
*   **Access Tokens:** TikTok API access tokens are stored securely and locally on your environment. You can revoke this access at any time directly through your TikTok account settings.

## 4. Third-Party Services

This Application integrates with the TikTok API. Your use of TikTok through this Application is governed by the [TikTok Terms of Service](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/terms-of-service) and [TikTok Privacy Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy).

## 5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
